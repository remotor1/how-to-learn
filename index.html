Teaching Techniques

5 practices by Barbara Oakley

  • Two kinds of attention

    Deep knowledge arises if you alternate between focused and diffuse thinking.

  • Recall

    Recall what you have learned - this will allow you to connect disparate portions of memory.

  • Interleaving

    Изучайте несколько навыков одновременно, они обогащают друг друга.

  • Questions

    While listening to the teacher, come up with a good question that really worries.

  • Illusion of competence

    Repeat to yourself, write it down, tell a friend: it only seems to you that you own a new theme.

The Barbara Oakley Story.

Since childhood, Barbara was not given mathematics. She considered herself complete humanist, and far from being the smartest. In the army, she studied Russian to get a raise, yeah so successful that she was promoted to commander. But for promotion, you had to take math. AND then Barbara came up with her own approach to the exact sciences. It turned out that if something is "bad for you", your the knowledge obtained by labor is much deeper than that of those to whom everything is clear at a glance.
